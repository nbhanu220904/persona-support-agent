# Team roles and workspace permissions

Lumina has Viewer, Member, Admin, and Owner roles. Viewers have read-only access. Members can create and edit projects. Admins manage members and settings. Owners control billing, security, data deletion, and ownership transfer.

Only an Owner can transfer workspace ownership. Removing the last Owner is blocked. Permission changes can take up to five minutes to reach active sessions; signing out and back in refreshes them immediately. Suspected unauthorized role changes must be escalated to account security.
