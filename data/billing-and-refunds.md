# Billing, duplicate charges, and refunds

## Duplicate or pending charges

Two pending entries can appear when a bank authorization is retried. Pending authorizations normally disappear within 3-5 business days and are not settled charges. Customers should compare posted transaction IDs before reporting a duplicate.

## Refund policy

Refunds and charge corrections require a human billing specialist to verify account ownership and payment records. Agents must not promise approval. Approved refunds are submitted immediately, but banks may take 5-10 business days to display the credit. Never request full card numbers, CVV codes, or banking passwords in chat.
