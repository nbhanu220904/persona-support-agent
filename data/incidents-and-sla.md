# Service incidents and SLA communication

## Incident lifecycle

Operational incidents move through investigating, identified, monitoring, and resolved states. The public status page is the source of truth. During an active incident, updates are posted at least every 30 minutes even when there is no material change.

## Business impact and SLA

Severity 1 means the production service is unavailable to most customers; the initial response target is 15 minutes. Severity 2 means major functionality is impaired; the initial response target is 60 minutes. Resolution time cannot be guaranteed before root cause is identified. SLA credit requests are reviewed after the incident report is published, normally within five business days of resolution.
