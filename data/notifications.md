# Email and notification delivery

Users control product, incident, and digest notifications under Profile > Notifications. Security alerts cannot be disabled. Email delivery can take up to five minutes.

If mail is missing, verify the address, inspect spam and quarantine, and allowlist `notifications@lumina.example`. Workspace administrators should allow SPF and DKIM-aligned messages from the Lumina sending domain. Support needs the approximate timestamp and notification type, but never mailbox credentials.
