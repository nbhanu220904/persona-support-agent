# Data export and retention

Workspace owners can start a data export from Settings > Data management. Exports are prepared asynchronously; a notification is sent when the encrypted archive is ready. Download links expire after 24 hours.

Deleted projects remain recoverable for 30 days, then enter permanent deletion. Audit logs are retained for 90 days on Standard and 365 days on Enterprise. Legal hold and custom retention requests require review by the privacy team and must be escalated.
